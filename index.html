Everything Erik ten Hag said in Manchester United press conference ahead of Chelsea fixture

·Erik ten Hag spoke to the press on Friday morning as he previewed Manchester United's upcoming trip to face Chelsea in the Premier League this weekend.

The United manager was asked about the ongoing Cristiano Ronaldo saga that has seen the Portuguese forward axed from the first-team and made to train alone ahead of their match at Stamford Bridge.

In terms of the actual match, Chelsea are winless in their last nine Premier League games against United, while six of the last eight meetings between the sides have finished level, including each of the last four in a row.

Ten Hag also issued some early team news for the encounter, with forward Anthony Martial still awaiting his return to action while club captain Harry Maguire made his return to training this week.
